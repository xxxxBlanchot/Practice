A brief introduction

Genshin Impact is an open-world ARPG (Action Role-Playing Game) developed by miHoYo, a Chinese video game development and animation studio. Currently, it supports platforms like Android, iOS, Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4&5, and it will release on Nintendo Switch in the future.


Stories of Genshin play out on the continent of Teyvat, a fantasy world with seven elements of power, including wind, water, fire, ice, rock, thunder, and grass. By far, there are over five countries and areas are open for players to explore, and more than 40 characters with different elemental power and weapon types to choose from. New Characters

On 11:00 AM (GMT+8), 5 January 2022, the new version (v2.4) named ‚ÄúFleeting Colours in Flight‚Äù was released. The information of the updated version includes several latest events, a new area Enkanomiya, two new characters who are five-star character Shenhe and four-star character Yunjin. I will focus on the discussion of the new characters on Twitter.


Shenhe

Even though she was born in the human world, she ended up being an adepti disciple. She grew up in the mountains far away from Liyue Harbor, her soul bound with red ropes, training both her body and mind.


Despite having the elegant temperament of an adeptus, she seems to be shrouded in mystery.


Yunjin

The current director of the Yun-Han Opera Troupe, a renowned Liyue opera singer who is skilled in both playwriting and singing. Her style is one-of-a-kind, exquisite and delicate, much like the person herself.
